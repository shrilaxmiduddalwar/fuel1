Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 28 August 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Fuel1, owned and operated by Behal Group (represented by Mr. Rajat Pal Singh), collects, uses, shares, and protects information from Customers, Petrol Pump Owners, and Partners who use our platform, website https://www.myfuel.info/, and related services (fuel, gas, diesel, and EV charging).

1. Information We Collect

Customer Information:

Name, mobile number, email, vehicle details (car/bike type, fuel type). Location data (optional, coming soon).

Petrol Pump Owner Information:

Name, mobile number, email, business details, pump location and fuel types.

Technical Information:

Device type, app usage logs.

Note: Aadhaar, payment gateway, and API data are not collected at this stage. These features will be added in the future with updated policies.

2. How We Use Information

  • Connect customers with nearby petrol pumps
  • Maintain customer‚Äìpump owner collaboration records
  • Share updates, alerts, and communication related to services
  • Improve app functionality and prepare for upcoming features (payments, APIs, etc.)

3. Data Sharing

With Petrol Pump Owners:

Customer's name, contact, and vehicle details may be shared for service delivery.

With Customers:

Petrol pump location, availability, and fuel type may be shared.

No Third-Party Sharing Yet:

Currently, no payment gateway or external API is connected.

4. Data Security

Basic data security practices are in place to protect personal details.

As features like payment gateway and APIs are added, stronger encryption and security protocols will be implemented.

5. User Rights

  • Customers and pump owners can request updates or deletion of their data by contacting Fuel1 support
  • Promotional messages can be opted out anytime

6. Data Retention

  • We retain only necessary customer and pump owner data for providing services
  • Accounts can be deleted on request

7. Third-Party Links

The app may show links to third-party services (future updates). We are not responsible for their privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Fuel1 services are not meant for users under 18 years without parental/guardian consent.

9. Changes to Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when new features (payment gateway, Aadhaar KYC, APIs) are introduced. Updates will be posted on www.myfuel.info.
